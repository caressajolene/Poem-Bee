
# Buz, Buz, Buz :honeybee: :honeybee:
###### By Clara Doty Bates 

Buz, Buz, Buz--says the Great buzzing Bee.

Go away butterfly--this flower is for me.

Why? Why? Why? says the little butterfly,

If you may sit on this flower, why may'nt I?
